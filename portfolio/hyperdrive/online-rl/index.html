Online Reinforcement Learning: Deep Dive

What is Online RL?

Online Reinforcement Learning (RL) involves learning policies directly through interaction with the environment, continuously updating strategies based on real-time feedback and exploration.

Key Characteristics

  • Direct environment interaction
  • Continuous policy adaptation
  • Exploration vs exploitation balance
  • Sample-efficient learning
